## 📚 About the project

**AeroSafe Quality** is a company specialized in consulting and advisory services for pharmaceutical, cosmetic, veterinary, and food industries, compounding pharmacies, and hospitals. It offers equipment qualification, process validation, and biological safety services, ensuring regulatory compliance and operational excellence.

## 🛠️ Tech stack

### Frontend
- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org) — React framework for web applications
- [React](https://reactjs.org) — UI library
- [TailwindCSS](https://tailwindcss.com) — Responsive styling
- [AOS (Animate On Scroll)](https://michalsnik.github.io/aos/) — Scroll-triggered animations
- [ShadCN](https://ui.shadcn.com) — Accessible UI components

### Infrastructure
- [Firebase Hosting](https://firebase.google.com/docs/hosting) — Hosting

## 💼 Services

- **Air treatment** — Maintenance and control of HVAC systems
- **Cleanrooms and controlled environments** — Certification and qualification of critical areas
- **Equipment qualification** — IQ, OQ, and PQ for regulatory compliance
- **Process validation** — Biological safety and quality in industrial settings
- **Industrial gas analysis** — Qualification of compressed air and other gases
